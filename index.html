Bolivia

Bolivia is a landlocked country in South America, bordered by Brazil to the north and east, Paraguay and Argentina to the south, and Chile and Peru to the west. It is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes, including the Andes mountains and the Amazon rainforest.
